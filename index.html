    About Me

    Education Section

    B.Tech in Computer Science and Business Systems (CSBS)
    Academy of Technology, West Bengal | 2023-2027

    When I began my journey in Computer Science and Business Systems (CSBS) at the Academy of Technology, West Bengal, I wasn’t sure which path to follow — AI/ML or Web Development. Like most first-year students, I explored both, learning the fundamentals of programming, logic, and problem-solving. Soon, I discovered where my true curiosity lies — in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Visualization. I became fascinated by how data, when analyzed mathematically, can tell powerful stories and even predict future trends. Since then, I’ve built a strong foundation in Python, SQL, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Scikit-learn, and DSA in C++ to strengthen my analytical thinking. I enjoy experimenting with models, visualizing complex datasets, and transforming raw information into meaningful insights. For me, AI isn’t just about algorithms — it’s about creating intelligent systems that make human lives easier. Every project I take on strengthens my belief that data has the power to drive positive change. Now, as a 3rd-year student, I’m exploring ML model deployment and data storytelling, aspiring to become a Machine Learning Engineer who blends logic, creativity, and purpose to build impactful solutions.
